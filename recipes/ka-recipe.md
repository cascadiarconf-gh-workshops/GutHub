# Easy Pancakes

## Ingredients
- 1 large egg
- 1/2 ripe banana

## Instructions
1. Blend egg and banana until smooth.
2. Heat oil in skillet over medium-high heat.
3. Cook 1-2 minutes on each side.
4. Enjoy!
